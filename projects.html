Projects & Activities

🎮 Speed Typing Invaders

A Python game I developed using Pygame, where players destroy falling words by typing them quickly. It helped me learn object-oriented programming, keyboard input handling, and game loop logic.

🧒 Coding with Kids Internship

I joined an internship where I helped younger students learn programming through fun activities. I learned how to communicate clearly, explain complex ideas simply, and be patient — which also helped me understand coding more deeply.

📜 Essay: The Meaning of "Good"

I wrote a short essay inspired by Nietzsche's quote that “good” is what makes the weak feel safe. I explored how morality is shaped by power, and what that means for modern ethical systems. This essay was a personal attempt to define “good” beyond emotion.
